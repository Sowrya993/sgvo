A Premier Educational Institution

The dream of Sri Gorantla Venkanna came to life on August 6, 1971, with the establishment of Sri Gorantla Venkanna Vidyapeetam by the Sri Gorantla Venkanna Trust. For 53 years, this esteemed institution has been dedicated to the promotion of classical languages like Telugu and Sanskrit, guiding countless students toward success. Renowned for its unique Gurukula-style education, the Vidyapeetam focuses on the holistic development of its students, fostering knowledge, values, and character.

Admissions Open

  • Five-year courses: For students who have completed their 10th grade, in B.A.O.L (Telugu) and B.A.O.L (Sanskrit).
  • Three-year courses: For those who studied Sanskrit as a second language in Intermediate, in B.A.O.L (Sanskrit).
  • Four-year course: For those who studied MPC in Intermediate, B.Sc. (Honours) Computer Science.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of these courses are eligible for various positions such as:

  • Government jobs: VRO, Panchayat Secretary, Police Constable, S.I, Groups – I, II, III, IV
  • Central Government UPSC jobs
  • Media: News Readers, Anchors, Journalists, Reporters
  • Translators and Teachers: Opportunities to work abroad for those proficient in Sanskrit.
